Part 2: Configure NTP
PC-A
Services → NTP
Enable Authentication
Key : 1
Password : NTPpa55

R1
enable
configure terminal

ntp server 192.168.1.5
ntp update-calendar

ntp authenticate
ntp trusted-key 1
ntp authentication-key 1 md5 NTPpa55

service timestamps log datetime msec
ex

show clock
show ntp status
